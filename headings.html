Dogs

Dogs are often called a mans best friend!

Cats

Cats spend up to 70% of their lives sleeping, which equates to roughly 16 hours a day. They are the ultimate energy-conservation masters

Horses

Did you know that horses physically cannot burp or vomit? Because their digestive system is a one-way street, gas buildup can't be released like it can in humans, making proper diet and digestion management absolutely critical for their health

Pigs

Pigs are incredibly intelligent creatures —research shows they can be smarter than dogs and possess a level of cognition similar to a three-year-old human child! They can even learn to use tools, recognize their own names, and play video games.

sparrow

House sparrows actually evolved alongside humans! Around 11,000 years ago, when early humans began farming grains, these tiny birds developed a special genetic mutation that allowed them to digest human agricultural crops.

Cows

Cows are highly social and intelligent animals that form lifelong friendships and experience a wide range of emotions. They are also surprisingly athletic, capable of running up to 25 mph and jumping up to 5 feet high.
