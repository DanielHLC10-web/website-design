Ingredients for fruit salad

  • Apple
  • Pear
  • Peach

Steps to make a fruit salad

  1. Take your fruits and cut them into bite size pieces
  2. Put them into a bowl
  3. Drizzle some lemon juice and put sorbet on top

Benefits of eating a fruit salad

Vitamins
Fruits are excellent natural sources of essential micronutrients, particularly Vitamin C, Vitamin A (via beta-carotene), Folate (Vitamin B9) , and Vitamin B6. These vitamins support everything from immune function and cell regeneration to heart health and metabolism.
